Recipe: Savory Apple Pork Chops

Indgredients:
	4 Pork Chops
	Salt
	Pepper
	2 Apples
	1/4 Ketchup
	Brown Sugar


Directions:
	Preheat Oven to 350 degrees
	Season Pork Chops
	Wrap Pork CHops in foil
	Arrange Apples around pork chops
	Brush Ketchup liberally
	Sprinkle brown sugar liberally
	Bake fro 35 to 45 minutes
