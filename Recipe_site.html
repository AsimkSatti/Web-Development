Savory Apple Pork Chops

Ingredients

  • 4 Pork Chops
  • Salt
  • 2 Apples
  • 1/4 Ketchup
  • Brown Sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Season Pork Chops
  3. Wrap Pork Chops in Foil
  4. Arrange Apples around Pork Chops
  5. Sprinkle Brown Sugar Liberally
  6. Bake for 35 to 45mins
